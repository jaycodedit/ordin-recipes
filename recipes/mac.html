Mac and Cheese

If you are looking for something quick and easy then this stove top mack and cheese is perfect for you! Mac and cheese is a classic comfort food made of creamy, velverty cheese sauce, and tender macaroni, creating a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • One forth cup butter
  • Half a teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook elblow macarioni in boiling water, stiring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite. roughly around 8 minutes.
  2. At the same time, melt butter in a sauce pan over medium heat
  3. Add flour, salt, pepper, and stir until smooth. Roughly 5 minutes.
  4. Pour milk in slowly while stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixure is smooth and bubbling. It should take around 5 minutes.
  5. Add cheddar cheese and stir until melted. It should take roughly 2 to 4 minutes
  6. Drain macarioni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
