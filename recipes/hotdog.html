Hot Dogs

A good hot dog is a savory delight that perfectly balances the flavors of its ingredients. Its perfect for a quick snack or a big meal!

ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Place buns in a single layer in the air fryer basket; cook for 2 minutes or until crisp. Remove buns to a plate
  3. Place hot dogs in a single layer in the air fryer basket; cook for 3 minutes. Serve hot dogs in toasted buns.
